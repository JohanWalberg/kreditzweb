Kreditz Business API

Let your existing and new customers share their financial data to you so you can make faster and better decisions. Complement your own data, or traditional credit data, with consumer contributed data through Kreditz Business API. Integrate Kreditz Business API directly into your application forms. Kreditz Business API is excellent for, amongst others:

  • Banks and financial institutions
  • Apartment rental sites
  • Insurance companies
  • Marketplaces (buy/sell/rent)
  • Recruiters and employers
  • Financial Identification
